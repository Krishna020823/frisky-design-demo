About Firsky

We're a bunch of colour-obsessed problem solvers.

Founded in 2016, Firsky Designs has grown from a two-person studio into a full-service creative team trusted by ambitious brands worldwide.

Our Story

Started with a sketchbook. Grew into a studio.

Firsky Designs began as a one-desk operation with a simple belief: good design should feel alive, not safe. Nine years later, that belief still runs through every project we touch.

Today we're a team of strategists, designers, and developers working with startups and established brands who want to look as bold as their ambitions.
